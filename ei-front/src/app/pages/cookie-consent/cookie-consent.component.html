Cookie Consent

Currently, the site does not store any of your personal information in cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, indexedDB, or any other local storage.

LocalStorage using

The site stores your search filter settings (keys «searchType», «searchRent», «searchSale») and the results of your last search query (key «searchResults») in localStorage. Key «invitation» keeps invitation code and status of its validation request. These data are stored until you clear your browser's cache or delete them selectively via the Developer Panel (cmd + opt + I for MacOS and F12 for Windows, Application -> Local storage).

Cookies using

In cookies, the site stores your selected site theme (key «{{ CookieNames.UserTheme }}», storage duration - 1 year) and the fact that you have accepted this cookie consent (keys «{{ CookieNames.CookieConsentAccepted }}» and «{{ CookieNames.CookieConsentAcceptanceDate }}», storage duration is also - 1 year). If we change this agreement, the keys «{{ CookieNames.CookieConsentAccepted }}» and «{{ CookieNames.CookieConsentAcceptanceDate }}» will be reset, and we will notify you that you need to review the cookie consent again.
